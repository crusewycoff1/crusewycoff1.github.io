About Tau Kappa Iota

Welcome to Tau Kappa Iota, a prestigious fraternity dedicated to fostering brotherhood, academic excellence, and the preservation of cherished traditions. Founded on February 29th, 2024, by visionary leaders M. Woodson Q. Hinrichs and C. Wycoff, our fraternity stands as a testament to the enduring spirit of camaraderie and the pursuit of shared ideals.

Our storied history traces its origins to a humble yet momentous gathering, where our illustrious founders, inspired by a fervent desire for fellowship and the spirit of adventure, conceived the bold vision of creating a fraternity unlike any other. From this visionary notion emerged Tau Kappa Iota, a bastion of tradition and a sanctuary where bonds of brotherhood are forged amidst the vibrant tapestry of life.

Guided by the sacred principles enshrined in our constitution, we celebrate the rich tapestry of diversity that defines our brotherhood. Through a myriad of academic pursuits, philanthropic endeavors, and social engagements, we strive to cultivate an environment where each member is empowered to realize their fullest potential and contribute meaningfully to the tapestry of our fraternity.

As proud stewards of our cherished legacy, we remain steadfast in our commitment to upholding the values of integrity, respect, and inclusivity that have guided Tau Kappa Iota since its inception. Together, we embark on a journey fueled by the boundless spirit of brotherhood, united in our quest to leave an indelible mark upon the annals of time.
