The year is 2025
War rages on in Europe as the Ukrainian Army
slowly pushes back the Russian invasion.
When the Ukranians were outside of Moscow,
they thought the war was won.
They were wrong.

The Russians had developed a device
they called "God's Hand"
It decimated everything in its path
Every stronghold, base, and outpost was destroyed.

Chernobyl is the last stronghold.
This will be Ukraine's final stand against the Russians
